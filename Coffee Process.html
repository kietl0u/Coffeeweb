Journey of Coffee Beans

Have you ever wondered, what journey has the fragrant cup of coffee in your hand gone through? When the waiter brings you a cup of coffee, it is just the final act of a long and magical journey: from choosing the right soil, sifting varieties, to planting, nurturing and harvest.

At Jame's Coffee House farm, we work side by side with each farmer, controlling the standards of the fertilization process; meticulous in the stages of preliminary processing, roasting, brewing... to send customers the best cup of coffee.
